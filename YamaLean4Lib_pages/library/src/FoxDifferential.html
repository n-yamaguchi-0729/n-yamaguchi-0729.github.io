71# Fox differential calculus
72
73Public entry point for the Fox-differential calculus library. The library contains crossed
74differentials, Fox derivatives on free groups and group rings, Fox boundaries, Jacobians,
75completed/profinite Fox differentials, and right Fox-derivative formulas.
76
77It may import `ProCGroups`, `ReidemeisterSchreier`, and `CompletedGroupAlgebra`. The downstream
78modules `CrowellExactSequence` and `MetabelianProductCentralizer` are intentionally not imported.
