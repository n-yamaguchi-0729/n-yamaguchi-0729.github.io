52# FenchelNielsenZomorrodian.Discrete
53

55period-coordinate data, quotient data, torsion frontiers, and the discrete main theorem used by
56the profinite bridge. Reduction proof data, relator proof certificates, and transport maps remain
57available through focused imports.
