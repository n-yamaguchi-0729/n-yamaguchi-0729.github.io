12# Bifiltered finite Fox stage transitions
13
14A finite Fox stage varies in two directions: the normal quotient `F/N` and the coefficient
15modulus. This file packages the combined transition
16
17`(Z/mZ)[F/N]^X ⋊ F/N ⟶ (Z/nZ)[F/M]^X ⋊ F/M`
18
19for `N ≤ M` and `n ∣ m`. This is the finite-stage interface needed for cofinal systems of
20simultaneous target refinements and prime-power coefficient reductions.
21-/
