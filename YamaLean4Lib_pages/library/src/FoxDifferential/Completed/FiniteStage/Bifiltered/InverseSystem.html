11/-!
12# Bifiltered inverse systems of finite Fox semidirect stages
13
14The completed density argument should eventually range over a cofinal family in which both the
15finite relation quotient and the coefficient modulus vary. The finite-stage files already provide
16single transition maps
17
18`(Z/m)[F/N]^X ⋊ F/N -> (Z/n)[F/M]^X ⋊ F/M`
19
20for `N ≤ M` and `n ∣ m`. This file upgrades those transitions to an honest inverse system for an
21arbitrary preorder-indexed family of normal subgroups and moduli. It also exposes the stagewise
22boundary-cycle subset and the compatible inverse-limit point attached to a single relation word.
23-/
