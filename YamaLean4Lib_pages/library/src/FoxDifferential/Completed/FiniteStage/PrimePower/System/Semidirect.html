12# Prime-power inverse system of finite Fox semidirect stages
13
14The completed density argument eventually needs honest finite quotients of
15`Z_C[[H]]^X ⋊ H`. For a fixed finite-stage normal subgroup `N`, this file constructs the
16coefficient-direction inverse system
17
18`((Z/ℓ^a)[F/N]^X ⋊ F/N)_a`
19
20and proves that its transition maps preserve the two finite objects used in the density proof:
21boundary-cycle points and kernel-word points.
22-/
