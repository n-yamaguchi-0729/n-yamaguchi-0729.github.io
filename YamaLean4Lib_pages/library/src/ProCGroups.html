74# ProCGroups
75
76Lightweight public root for reusable profinite and pro-`C` group infrastructure.
77
78This root keeps the default import surface small: finite-group classes, pro-`C` predicates,
79profinite groups, open normal quotients, inverse systems, categorical pullbacks/pushouts,
80generation, finite generation, and finite-step solvable quotients.
81
82Advanced topics such as completions, free pro-`C` groups, free-product universal properties,
83presentations, local-weight theory, normal-subgroup structure, duality, Frattini theory, and
84wreath products are available from `ProCGroups.Advanced` or from their focused folder wrappers.
85-/
