28/-- A computation-facing variant of
29`CleanedReidemeisterSchreierTargetPresentationData`. Instead of asking
30directly for
31`FreeGroup.lift toTargetGenerator (cleanedTau q r) ≡ 1`, it asks for a concrete
32target word and a proof that substituting the target-generator words into the
33reduced `cleanedTauNormalWord q r` reduces to that target word. -/
