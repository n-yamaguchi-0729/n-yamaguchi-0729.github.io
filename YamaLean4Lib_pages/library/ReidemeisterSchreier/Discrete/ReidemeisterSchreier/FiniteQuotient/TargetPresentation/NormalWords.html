A computation-facing variant of \(\mathrm{CleanedReidemeisterSchreierTargetPresentationData}\). Instead of asking directly for \(\mathrm{FreeGroup.lift}(\mathrm{toTargetGenerator},\mathrm{cleanedTau}(q,r)) \equiv 1\), it asks for a concrete target word and a proof that substituting the target-generator words into the reduced \(\mathrm{cleanedTauNormalWord}(q,r)\) reduces to that target word.
