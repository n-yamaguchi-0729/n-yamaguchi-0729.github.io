theorem threeStep_sourceSubgroup_exists_of_zeroGenus_periodData
    (σ : FuchsianSignature)
    (hZero : σ.orbitGenus = 0)
    (D : FirstReductionPeriodData σ) :
    ∃ L : Subgroup (FuchsianPresentedGroup σ),
      L.FiniteIndex ∧ IsTorsionFreeGroup L ∧
        SubgroupQuotientHasDerivedLengthAtMost L 3

Zero-genus compact three-step construction from explicit period data. This is the proof-carrying discrete main theorem used by the profinite bridge. It avoids re-running the perfectness-to-period-data conversion when the profinite side has already produced the required shared-prime pair.
