theorem not_pairwiseCoprimeFamily_iff_exists_prime_dvd_two
    {ι : Type*} {periods : ι → ℕ} :
    (¬ ∀ i j, i ≠ j → Nat.Coprime (periods i) (periods j)) ↔
      ∃ p : ℕ, p.Prime ∧
        ∃ i j : ι, i ≠ j ∧ p ∣ periods i ∧ p ∣ periods j

A period family is not pairwise coprime exactly when some prime divides two distinct periods.
